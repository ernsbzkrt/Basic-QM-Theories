# 🚀Fundamental Concepts of Quantum Mechanics

##  🌠Overview
*This repository explores Fundamental Concepts of Quantum Mechanics with the aim of laying a theoretical groundwork for Quantum Computing.*

## ⚛️Introduction
**The topics to be examined within this section are listed below. Each topic will be discussed in depth under its own heading:**

- Quantum States: Qubits and Quantum Superposition
- Entanglement, Bell Inequalities, and Teleportation
- Quantum Measurement: Collapse Theory and the Copenhagen Interpretation
- Quantum Tunneling and Its Applications in Computing
